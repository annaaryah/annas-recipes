  • 2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 cup packed brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 cups semisweet chocolate chips

Steps‚ú®

  1. Preheat the Oven: Preheat your oven to 375¬∞F (190¬∞C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. Combine Dry Ingredients: In a medium-sized bowl, whisk together the all-purpose flour and baking soda. Set aside.
  3. Cream Butter and Sugars: In a large mixing bowl, cream together the softened butter, granulated sugar, brown sugar, and vanilla extract until the mixture is light and fluffy.
  4. Add Eggs: Add the eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition. This creates a smooth, creamy base for your cookie dough.
  5. Incorporate Dry Ingredients: Gradually add the dry ingredient mixture to the wet ingredients, mixing until just combined. Be careful not to overmix; you want a soft, tender cookie.
  6. Fold in Chocolate Chips: Gently fold in the semisweet chocolate chips, distributing them evenly throughout the cookie dough. This step is where the magic happens!
  7. Scoop Dough onto Baking Sheet: Use a cookie scoop or a spoon to drop rounded portions of dough onto the prepared baking sheet. Ensure they are spaced at least 2 inches apart to allow for spreading.
  8. Bake to Golden Perfection: Bake in the preheated oven for 9 to 11 minutes or until the edges are golden brown. The centers may still look slightly underdone ‚Äì that's okay; they'll continue to set as they cool.
  9. Cool on Wire Rack: Allow the cookies to cool on the baking sheet for a few minutes before transferring them to a wire rack to cool completely.
  10. Indulge and Enjoy: Once cooled, your chocolate chip cookies are ready to be devoured. Grab a glass of milk and savor the deliciousness!
